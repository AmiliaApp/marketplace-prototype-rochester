Tumbling

Junior High and High School, Tuesdays and Wednesdays from 7:30pm to 8:30pm

Start: Mon. February 6

Facility : YMCA Saint-Laurent - 1745 rue Décarie

Description : Basic beginner through advanced tumbling on floor, trampoline, and tumble-trak are covered, depending upon skill level of gymnast.

Spots available: Full

Price: $215 per 9-week session

Age restrictions: Junior High and High School

Gender: Boys and girls

Prerequisits: None.

Please Be Prepared for Class!
  • A one-piece leotard is required for girls (tumblers and gymnasts, both). Gym shorts or Lycra dance pants may be worn over leotard. If wearing tights, they must be footless.
  • Hair must be pulled back securely. Whatever works best for your hair is fine… ponytail, pigtails. Long bangs should be pulled back also. Snap clips work best.
  • No Jewelry, Please. If ears are pierced, small post earrings are allowed but nothing that goes beneath the ear lobe.
  • Boys should wear a tucked in t-shirt with gym shorts, no belts, buckles, zippers or snaps. Cotton socks on the feet are fine for some events.

All registration fees are non-refundable. We give no class tuition refunds after a session begins except to Mini Olympians new to the program.
